# Remote Work Policy

## Eligibility

All full-time employees who have completed their 3-month probation period are eligible for hybrid remote work. Employees work from the office on Monday, Tuesday, and Thursday; Wednesday and Friday may be worked remotely.

## Equipment

The company provides a laptop, monitor, and a one-time home office budget of 2,000 PLN for eligible employees. Equipment requests go through the IT service desk and require manager approval.

## Working Hours

Core hours are 10:00–15:00 CET, during which employees must be reachable. Outside core hours, employees may flex their schedule as long as they complete 8 working hours per day.

## Security Requirements

Remote work requires connecting through the corporate VPN. Confidential documents must not be stored on personal devices. Video calls discussing client data must be taken from a private room, not public spaces.
