# Expense Reimbursement Policy

## What Can Be Expensed

Business travel (flights, trains, hotels), client meals up to 200 PLN per person, professional certifications and conference tickets approved by your manager, and software licenses required for project work.

## How to Submit

Submit expenses through the finance portal within 30 days of the purchase date. Attach the original receipt or invoice — credit card statements alone are not accepted. Expenses above 1,000 PLN require pre-approval from your department head.

## Reimbursement Timeline

Approved expenses are reimbursed with the next monthly payroll run. Expenses submitted after the 15th of the month roll over to the following payroll.

## Training Budget

Each engineer has an annual training budget of 4,000 PLN covering courses, certifications, and books. Unused budget does not roll over to the next year.
