# Procurement Policy

## Raising a Purchase Order

Any purchase of goods or services on behalf of the company starts with a purchase order in the procurement system. Do not commit to a vendor or sign an order form before the purchase order is approved.

## Quotes and Vendor Selection

Purchases above 10,000 PLN require at least three written quotes attached to the purchase order. New vendors must pass a security and data-protection review before their first order is placed.

## Software and Licenses

Software licenses and SaaS subscriptions require sign-off from the IT architecture team in addition to the budget owner, to avoid duplicate tools and check integration and security requirements.

## Renewals

Recurring subscriptions are reviewed 30 days before renewal. Owners must confirm the tool is still in use; unclaimed renewals are cancelled.
