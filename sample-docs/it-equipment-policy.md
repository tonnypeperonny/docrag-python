# IT Equipment Policy

## Standard Issue

New employees receive a laptop, an external monitor, a keyboard, and a mouse on their first day. Requests for a second monitor or a docking station go through the IT service desk and need manager approval.

## Refresh Cycle

Laptops are replaced every three years, or sooner if a hardware fault cannot be repaired. The old device must be returned to IT before the replacement is handed over so it can be wiped and re-imaged.

## Asset Tracking

Every device carries an asset tag and is recorded in the inventory system against the employee it is assigned to. Report a lost or damaged device to the IT service desk the same day.

## Return on Leaving

All company equipment is returned on the last working day. Unreturned equipment is deducted from the final settlement at replacement cost.
