# Travel Booking Policy

## Booking Channel

All business travel must be booked through the corporate travel portal, not with personal accounts. Flights, trains, and hotels booked outside the portal are not guaranteed reimbursement even with a valid receipt.

## Class and Limits

Economy class is standard for flights under six hours; business class needs director approval. Hotels are capped at 500 PLN per night in Poland and 700 PLN per night abroad. A daily meal per diem of 150 PLN applies while travelling and does not require individual receipts.

## Approval

Trips with a total estimated cost above 5,000 PLN require director sign-off before booking. Same-day domestic trips under 500 PLN can be booked without prior approval.

## Changes and Cancellations

Change or cancel through the travel portal as early as possible. Cancellation fees caused by personal reasons are not reimbursed; fees from business schedule changes are.
