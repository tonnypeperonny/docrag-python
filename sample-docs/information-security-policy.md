# Information Security Policy

## Accounts and Authentication

Every employee account uses multi-factor authentication. Passwords must be at least 14 characters and are rotated every 180 days. Shared accounts are prohibited; access is always tied to a named person.

## Data Classification

Documents are classified as public, internal, or confidential. Confidential data may only be stored in approved company systems and must never be copied to personal devices or personal cloud storage.

## Device Security

Company laptops have full-disk encryption enabled and receive security patches automatically. A lost or stolen device must be reported to the security team within 24 hours so it can be wiped remotely.

## Incident Reporting

Suspected phishing or a data breach must be reported to security@ immediately. Do not attempt to investigate or clean up an incident yourself; preserve evidence and wait for the security team.
